# 🌿 Project Evergreen Vision

> **Build the most beautiful digital home for every animal that matters to someone.**

Project Evergreen is not only a pet tracker. It is a home for:

- 🐾 Pets
- 🐦 Wild Friends
- 🆘 Rescues
- 🏡 Fosters
- 🌈 Memorials

## Milestones

### 🌱 Milestone 1 — Foundation
Profiles, care logs, galleries, reminders, backups and polished navigation.

### 🪵 Milestone 2 — Habitat
A Sims-style habitat builder with real assets, measurements, layers, constraints and saved layouts.

### ❤️ Milestone 3 — Life
Growth charts, medical timelines, feeding insights, memories, relationships and milestones.

### 🤖 Milestone 4 — Intelligence
Helpful habitat checks, care suggestions, product recognition and pattern insights.

### 🌍 Milestone 5 — Community
Shared habitats, inspiration, rescue stories and animal memories.

## Evergreen DNA

Every feature should be:

- Beautiful
- Helpful for animals
- Easy to use
- Something we would genuinely use ourselves
